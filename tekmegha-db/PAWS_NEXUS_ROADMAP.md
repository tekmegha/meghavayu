- `pet_emergency_contacts` - Emergency services

- `LayoutPetCare` - Pet care specific layout
- `PetCareServicesComponent` - Services and doctors
- Dynamic routing system
- Mobile-first design

### Integration Points
- Supabase backend
- Payment processing
- SMS/Email notifications
- Analytics tracking
